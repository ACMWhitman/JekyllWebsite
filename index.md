# Welcome to Whitman ACM

The Whitman College chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is a community of creative technologists, designers, and interdisciplinary thinkers.  
We explore computer science, data, art, and sound through projects, talks, and workshops.

## What We Do
- Build projects that blend computing and creativity  
- Host student-led workshops and talks with free pizza
- Collaborate with the Walla Walla community on different projects
